Key Characteristics of Velociraptor:

[1] Size: Velociraptors were relatively small dinosaurs, measuring about 2 feet (0.6 meters) in height and 6.8 feet (2.1 meters) in length. They were about the size of a turkey.

[2] Feathers: Velociraptors were feathered dinosaurs. Fossils have revealed evidence of feathers, suggesting that they were covered in a layer of feathers, which may have served for insulation or display purposes.

>

[3] Sharp Claws: Velociraptors had a large, sickle-shaped claw on each foot, especially on their second toe. These claws were used for grasping and possibly slashing at prey.

[4] Carnivorous Diet: Velociraptors were carnivorous predators, and their diet likely consisted of small vertebrates, such as other dinosaurs or early mammals.

[5] Agile and Fast: They were agile and probably capable of quick bursts of speed, making them effective hunters.

[6] Intelligent: Velociraptors had relatively large brains for dinosaurs, suggesting they were intelligent and may have had complex social behaviors.
